05 / How I work 過程

A senior process focused on risk, evidence and delivery.

  1. 01Frame the product problem

    Clarify the user, business goal, constraints and decision criteria.

  2. 02Map users, systems and dependencies

    Expose complexity before simplifying it.

  3. 03Identify high-risk assumptions

    Prioritise what needs evidence, not just what needs screens.

  4. 04Prototype the critical path

    Make the core journey testable and discussable quickly.

  5. 05Design for scale and edge cases

    Account for states, errors, permissions, handoffs and operational reality.

  6. 06Partner through delivery

    Keep design quality intact as the product moves into build and iteration.
